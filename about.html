About us

Who we are.

SUN-ARINU is Uganda's official Scaling Up Nutrition network of academic and research institutions — mobilizing collective expertise to end malnutrition in all its forms by 2030.

Kampala · Uganda
Home/About

A joint platform for Uganda's nutrition researchers.

SUN-ARINU was formed on 8 June 2023, spearheaded by the Office of the Prime Minister and the Ministry of Education and Sports. It was formally launched on 17 May 2024 during the National Nutrition Forum in Kampala.

It operates as one of Uganda's official SUN Movement networks under the Uganda Nutrition Action Plan (UNAP II), alongside the SUN Business Network, SUN Civil Society Network, and Development Partners Group.

The network harnesses the collective expertise of universities, researchers, and research institutions — turning nutrition science into training, policy, and practice that reaches communities across Uganda's four regions.
